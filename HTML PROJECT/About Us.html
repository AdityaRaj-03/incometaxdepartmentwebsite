Income Tax Department in India

The Income Tax Department of India has control over the functioning of the following Directorates which are headed by the directors of Income Tax and report through the Pr.DGIT(admin.) to the Central Board of Direct Taxes and read as under:

	1.Directorate of Income Tax (Public Relations,Printing Publications and Official Language)
	2.Directorate of Income Tax(Income Tax) - (Inspection and Examination)
	3.Directorate of Income Tax(Audit)
	4.Directorate of Income Tax(Recovery)
	5.Directorate of Income Tax(TDS)
